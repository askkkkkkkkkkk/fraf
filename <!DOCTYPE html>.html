Your support can make a big difference for the community in Sitio Bebe. Every contribution goes towards improving living conditions, providing essential resources, and creating a better future.

Chosen Category

We specifically chose the "Educational" category as it aligns with our objective to create content that is informative, enlightening, and inspirational. By focusing on this category, we aim to impart knowledge and insights that can positively impact the lives of our audience, especially students. We believe that our content not only serves the purpose of creation but also contributes valuable information that can benefit and empower our viewers.

Target Audience

Since our content aims to help the unfortunate, we think that it would be best if our target audience would be teens, people who are willing to help the unfortunate, and sponsors or brands that are willing enough to help the unfortunate. Teens will be such a great help to our content since they are mostly the ones that like to speak up for things. With today's teenagers, so many young people create content on TikTok and YouTube. With their help and influence, we can expand our content more.

YouTube Channel Setup

Choosing a compelling channel name that reflects our mission. Creating a visually appealing channel banner and profile picture. Writing a clear and concise channel description that outlines our goals and target audience.

Content Creation

Starting brainstorming specific video ideas based on the content types we discussed. Plan out our video scripts and gather any necessary resources. Recording our initial videos and experiment with editing styles.

Marketing and Promotion

Creating a social media strategy to promote our channel. Sharing our videos on relevant online communities and forums.

Why We Need to Address?

In Sitio Bebe, we need to address the lack of access to clean water, healthcare professionals, and essential health resources. Our YouTube channel can become a platform for raising awareness, sharing stories of resilience, and inspiring viewers to donate to support these needs. We can create videos showcasing the challenges faced by the community, highlighting the impact of clean water access and healthcare, and providing practical ways for viewers to contribute. By focusing on empathy and action, we can empower viewers to make a difference for Sitio Bebe.
